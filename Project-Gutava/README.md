# 🥤 Case Study: GUTAVA (Pink Guava Soft-Chew)
### "Balanced Gut, Trusted Wellness" — A Data-Driven Brand Strategy

---

## 📌 Executive Summary
**GUTAVA** was an end-to-end journey of identifying a market gap and transforming it into a validated product strategy. This project goes beyond a classroom assignment, demonstrating my ability to manage a brand's lifecycle from initial research to systematic risk management.

---

## 🔍 1. Identifying the Market Gap
While the **Gut Health** sector was booming, I observed a lack of credible, lifestyle-oriented local brands. I decided to dive deep into three specific consumer segments:
* **Gen X & Y:** Driven by a **"Preventive Lifestyle"** and long-term health.
* **Gen Z:** Driven by **"Holistic Wellness"** and skin-related benefits.

---

## 🧪 2. Concept Testing & Data Validation
To stress-test the brand's viability, I conducted a **Concept Test with 30 target users**. This phase was crucial for moving beyond assumptions:

* **Methodology:** Feedback analysis using **Best, Normal, and Weak scenarios**.
* **The Turning Point:** While Gen Z initially focused on skin benefits, the data revealed that **"Gut Health Efficiency"** and **"Convenience"** were the absolute non-negotiable deal-breakers for all demographics.

---

## 🎨 3. Visual Identity & Brand Direction
I refined the entire brand direction based on research insights, leading to the brand mantra: **"Balanced Gut, Trusted Wellness."** The visual identity was designed to bridge the gap between traditional health credibility and modern aesthetic appeal.

## 💡 4. Strategic Innovation & Solution
Based on the validation results, I prioritized two key elements:
* **Low-Friction Format:** Developed a **Soft-chew** format to meet the "Convenience" requirement for busy, on-the-go lifestyles.
* **Local Ingredient Strategy:** Sourced local **"Phu Phirun" Pink Guava**, providing high-quality prebiotics while supporting local farmers and sustainability.

---

## 🎓 Key Takeaways & Competencies
This project gave me the confidence to:
1.  **Synthesize raw insights** from different generations into a solid brand blueprint.
2.  **Stress-test creative ideas** using systematic risk management.
3.  **Execute end-to-end strategy** that aligns user needs with market feasibility.
